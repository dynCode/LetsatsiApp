Letsatsi Lifestyle

What Is Letsatsi Lifestyle?

  • Letsatsi Lifestyle is a lifestyle programme powered by Mahala. The programme is aimed at rewarding you for being a loyal Letsatsi Finance and Loan (Pty) Ltd (Letsatsi Finance) client and forming good credit behaviour.
  • It will also help you accumulate points which you can use to spoil yourself at a number of great retailers and suppliers.
  • All members will be able to earn loyalty points for every successful loan repayment.
  • The loyalty points can be used for the following - Point partners where you can redeem points and discount partners where you can enjoy up to 50% discounts within the Mahala partner network.

How do I apply to Letsatsi Lifestyle?

  • Visit any of Our Letsatsi branches nationwide.
  • Please visit www.letsatsilifestyle.co.za to find out where your closest branch is.
  • You can email lifestyle@letsatsifinance.co.za for any application queries.

How does Letsatsi Lifestyle work?

  • Letsatsi Lifestyle comprises of 2 packages.
  • Gold members and Diamond members.
  • In order to apply for the Gold status membership you will need to have an active loan at Letsatsi Finance and be a loyal Letsatsi Finance client.

What if I do not have an active loan?

  • You can join Letsatsi Lifestyle as a Diamond status member at R120.00 (one hundred and twenty rand) per month.
  • The membership of a Gold status member who does not have an active loan with Letsatsi Finance for more than 6 months, will be dormant.

How do I accumulate points?

Membership

When will my membership be activated?

  • Your membership will be activated with 8 (eight) working days of Mahala’s receipt of Your application, should Your application be successfully processed.
  • Diamond status benefits will be activated on receipt of payment of the first successful monthly membership fee of R120.00(one hundred and twenty rand) paid by the member. You can start swiping your card for point’s accrual from DAY 1 but will only be able to redeem as soon as the first Membership fee was received.

How long is my membership valid?

  • Gold status membership is valid when the member has an active loan with Letsatsi Finance and Loan (Pty) Ltd.
  • If a Gold status member does not have an active loan for more than 6 months, there membership will become dormant.
  • When you take out a new loan thereafter, your membership will be reactivated.
  • You will continue to use the same loyalty card as this is linked to you.
  • Diamond status membership will be valid upon successful payments of the R120.00 monthly membership fee.

What happens when I miss a payment?

  • If You as a Diamond status member have not paid Your monthly Membership fees for 2 (two) consecutive months, or should the second attempt to debit the membership fee by debit order not be successful, Your membership will automatically be suspended. You will still be able to accrue points, however You will not be able to use any points until your membership has been re-activated.

How do I use the Credit Know benefit?

This membership benefit is provided by Letsatsi to Diamond status members. To utilize this benefit please send an email to Lifestyle@letsatsifinance.co.za or contact Your nearest Letsatsi branch www.letsatsilifestyle.co.za.

  • You can request Your credit report every 4 (four) months (being 3 (three) times per year) Mondays to Fridays from 8:00am to 5:00pm and on Saturdays from 8:00am to 12:00pm. Credit Know is not available on Sundays and public holidays.
  • A copy of Your personal credit report can be collected at Your nearest Letsatsi branch or this can be emailed to You once you provide an email address and lodge a request for Your personal credit report.
  • A Letsatsi consultant will have to verify Your personal information prior to processing Your request, and You will need to present a copy of your ID and Letsatsi lifestyle membership card on collection of Your credit report.
  • If there are any adverse listing alerts on Your credit record, these will be sms’d to you in the first week of each month. Adverse listings refer to, inter alia, Judgments taken against You, reports of late or non-payments made by You, or collection notice listings (debt collection or administration orders). If You would like more information regarding Your sms alerts, please send your queries to lifestyle@letsatsifinance.co.za; please provide your full name, ID number, contact number and reason with such request.
  • You are entitled to set up a meeting with a Letsatsi Consultant in which:
    1. The meeting will be up to a maximum of 30 (thirty) minutes per meeting.
    2. The meetings are limited to a maximum of 3 (three) meetings per year.
    3. The Letsatsi consultant will guide You through Your credit report, explaining what each section means, as well as assist You in putting together a basic budget.
    4. Meetings can only be arranged in the middle two weeks of the month, i.e. 8th to the 22nd of each month.
    5. You can arrange a mutually suitable meeting time with your Letsatsi Branch consultant in a branch of Letsatsi.

Who is considered a partner and child for cover under the R10 000 Funeral Benefit?

  • A Partner is a person married to the Diamond member by Civil Law, Common Law, Tribal or Religious Custom and includes same sex partners.
  • A Child includes all unmarried biological Children of the Diamond member or a Child of the Diamond member through guardianship in terms of Civil, Common or Customary law. Unmarried mentally disabled, permanently and totally disabled Children are included until their 25th birthday or until the Diamond member’s membership is deactivated, whichever event occurs first

Is there a waiting period on the R10 000 Funeral Benefit?

  • A 6 (six) month waiting period applies from date of Diamond membership activation in respect of death due to natural causes for all Insured People disclosed at inception of the Membership.
  • The waiting period for any of Your immediate family members added thereafter, will start the 6 (six) waiting period from the first of the month following Your notification to Mahala.
  • No waiting period applies in respect of death due to accidental causes.

How do I claim for my R10 000 Funeral Benefit?

No claims will be considered if reported more than 6 (six) months after the date of death.

  • Claims Procedures: The Diamond member or the deceased’s family members may contact Hollard Life on 0860-101- 003 for immediate 24 hour claims assistance.
  • The beneficiary is advised to go directly to an undertaker on death of the deceased Diamond member, and ask the undertaker to contact Hollard Life directly.
  • On valid claims Hollard Life can pay all expenses up to the cover level directly to the Undertaker once a detailed invoice and written authorisation from the beneficiary have been received. No cash deposit is needed. Alternatively a cash benefit may be requested.
  • The following documentation is required and must be forwarded to Hollard Life for claims processing:
    1. A fully completed claim form;
    2. A certified copy of the identity document of the claimant/beneficiary;
    3. A certified copy of the identity document and death certificate of the deceased Insured Person and a copy of the form BI1663/DHA1663;
    4. In the case of the Partner, a certified copy of the marriage certificate or certified proof of the common law or traditional marriage;
    5. In the case of a biological child, a certified copy of the Member’s or Partner’s identity document, or proof of relationship, and the child’s birth certificate;
    6. In case of dependent child aged 21 (twenty one) years up to but not including 25 (twenty five) years, and enrolled as a full-time student at a registered educational institution, certified proof of schooling;
    7. In the case of a disabled child, proof of disability;
    8. A “police report” in the event of death by unnatural causes;
    9. All copies must be certified by a commissioner of oaths.
